Services and Fees

What's it like to work with me? It's all about discovering where you are out and meeting you there. In the end every single decision is 100% yours to make. Your comfort level with pacing and strategies is paramount.

I offer free initial consultations. This consists of 3 basic parts:

1. Up to 30 minutes of talking with you and understanding your concerns and questions.

2. A review of the pertinent records regarding your student (school evaluations, IEP/504, medical diagnosis/es information), etc.

3. A follow up call to discuss the issues and potential strategies moving forward. My suggestions will be the result of my assessment of your student's records as well as your concerns.

My fee is $40 per hour; $20 per half hour

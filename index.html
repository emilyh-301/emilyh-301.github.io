Please be patient while this website is under construction, thank you!

If you have reached this website chances are you're experiencing some concerns with your student in school. I have always said that parents or guardians don't reach out to me when things are going well and I’m OK with that because I'm here to help. Navigating the world of special education is not easy.

The analogy I always use is that parents are walking into the school district knowing how to play checkers but the school is playing chess. Think about that. Chess is far more complicated, has a lot of rules and requires strategy. I see it as my job to teach you how to play chess to at least level the playing field. Oftentimes I find that the problem starts with parents not knowing what they don't know. First of all, special education lingo is a language all unto itself.
